About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Habi Factory has established itself as one of Egypt's premier wood product manufacturers. With over10 years of experience, we've grown from a small Cairo workshop to serving clients across Egypt and the Middle East.

We specialize in B2B wood products for contractors, furniture manufacturers, and homeowners. Our extensive range includes custom components, doors, flooring, raw materials, kitchen cabinets, outdoor furniture, and decorative items—all crafted with precision and care.

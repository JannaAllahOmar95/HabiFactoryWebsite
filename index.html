Wooden Doors Project for Al-Rowad Company

Modern Engineering – R5 Residential Project | Internal & External Wooden Door Works

This project involves the manufacture, supply, and installation of high-quality wooden doors for the R5 Residential Development in Egypt’s New Administrative Capital, under a formal subcontract agreement with ROWAD Modern Engineering. The scope includes 150 external doors and 300 internal doors, executed in full compliance with the approved specifications, shop drawings, and contractual requirements outlined in the project documentation.

All doors are produced using engineered wooden structures with MDF finishes, designed to meet durability, dimensional accuracy, and aesthetic consistency standards. The works cover complete door assemblies, including leaf construction, frames, architraves, surface finishing, and hardware preparation, ensuring seamless integration with architectural and interior design requirements. Execution follows approved measurements, quality controls, and installation procedures to guarantee performance and long-term reliability.

Fayoum Hospital Project: Safety and Durability in Healthcare

In our project with Fayoum Hospital, we ensured that the doors were a model of absolute quality, high security, and exceptional durability. Given the sensitive nature of the medical environment, we used internationally certified materials that guarantee the highest levels of safety and protection. These doors are specifically designed to be fire-resistant, utilizing heat-insulating and flame-retardant materials in accordance with international safety standards, providing the hospital with a vital first line of defense against emergencies.

Beyond safety, we prioritized practical functionality; the doors are designed for effortless operation and rapid movement for medical teams, featuring special finishes that make them easy to clean and sanitize for a healthy environment. They are also equipped with internal metal reinforcements to ensure high resistance to the continuous, heavy usage required in a 24/7 medical facility. This project embodies our vision of providing integrated solutions for the healthcare sector, merging safety and quality to support operational efficiency and peace of mind for patients and staff alike.
